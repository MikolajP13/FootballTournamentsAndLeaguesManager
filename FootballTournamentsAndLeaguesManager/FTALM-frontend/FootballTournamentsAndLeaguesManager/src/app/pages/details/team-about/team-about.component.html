This module is designed for managing team and viewing various aspects of team activity.

  • Users can view the current matches in which a team is participating.
  • Information about past competitions is available if the tournament or league exists.
  • Users are granted the capability to add players to a team, explore player data including their positions, designate team captains, edit and remove players from the team.
  • The statistics sub-page provides users with the ability to review club statistics. This includes goal differentials, summaries of wins, draws, losses, as well as the total number of matches played in competitions.
  • Moreover, the statistic page showcases the ongoing statistics within the league or tournament in which the team is engaged, offering insights into the current performance and standings.
  • You can only delete teams that are not currently participating in any leagues or tournaments.
