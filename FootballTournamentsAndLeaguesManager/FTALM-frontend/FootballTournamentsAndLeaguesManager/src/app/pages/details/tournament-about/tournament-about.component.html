This module is designed for creating and managing tournaments

  • Users can view the current tournament matches and fill out match protocols.
  • Users can add teams to the tournament, explore team data, review past matches, and access to statistics.
  • The tournament bracket sub-page provides detailed information about the tournament structure.
  • The matches sub-page presents general match data and offers detailed information when users click on the chevron icon.
  • Moreover, the statistics page showcases ongoing tournament statistics.
  • You can only delete tournaments that are not currently in progress.
  • To activate a tournament and initiate processes like matching teams and creating a tournament bracket, click the 'Start tournament' button.
