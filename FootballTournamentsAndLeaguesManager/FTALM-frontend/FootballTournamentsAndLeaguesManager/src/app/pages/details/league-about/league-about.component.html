This module is designed for creating and managing leagues

  • Users can view the current league matches and fill out match protocols.
  • Users can add teams to the league, explore team data, review past matches, and access to statistics.
  • The league table sub-page provides detailed information about the league table.
  • The matches sub-page presents general match data and offers detailed information when users click on the chevron icon.
  • Moreover, the statistics page showcases ongoing league statistics.
  • You can only delete leagues that are not currently in progress.
  • To activate a league and initiate processes like matching teams and creating a league table, click the 'Start league' button.
